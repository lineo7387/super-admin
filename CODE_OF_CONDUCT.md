# Code of Conduct

Super Admin should be a useful, welcoming project for people building admin tools.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections.
- Keep feedback specific to the code, design, documentation, or process.
- Help newcomers understand the project boundary before asking them to rewrite large pieces.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests.
- Publishing private or security-sensitive information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove harmful content and may restrict participation when needed to protect the project.
